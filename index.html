A little bit more about me

Trained in graphic design, I’m passionate about making things look better. These days I mostly work as a digital designer. This means that while I’m great with Photoshop, Illustrator or InDesign, I’m also comfortable using HTML and CSS.

I have extensive experience as a designer and art director, first at Soap Creative then Redbox Digital, before moving onto various freelance work. I’m always on the lookout for new projects, so feel free to email me.
