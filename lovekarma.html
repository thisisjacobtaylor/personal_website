LoveKarma

2014

Branding for LoveKarma organic foods. A small operation offering homemade organic treats, LoveKarma is all about sustainable living and reconnecting with nature. The logo is a modern take on historical symbols for the cycle of karma. While the use of an ink stamp for collateral was chosen to reduce the environmental costs associated with printing.
